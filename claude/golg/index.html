Geometry of Linear Groups

Welcome to an interactive exploration of linear groups and their geometric, algebraic, and arithmetic properties. This collection of tools is designed to help visualize and compute various aspects of linear groups, from their action on trees and buildings to their arithmetic subgroups and representation varieties.
