Resch Games

Modern HTML5 ports of Richard Evan Schwartz's classic mathematical interactive experiences, originally developed as Java applets.

Lucy & Lily II

A mesmerizing polygon-flipping puzzle. Flip regular polygons across their edges to navigate a world of symmetry and return the scrambled pieces to their goal.

Play Now

The √2 Game

Navigate a kayak through the lattice of the Z[√2] ring. Master movements of length √2±1 and explore the dense structure of algebraic number fields.

Explore Grid

Drivin' Me Crazy

A steering game set in hyperbolic 3-space. Maneuver through a space of expanding pseudoregular hexagons and prevent the inner boundary from collapsing.

Take the Wheel

Simon & Lily

A deep exploration of arithmetic lattices in H2 × H2. Control a hyperbolic octagon in Simon's view and watch its Galois conjugate dance in Lily's world.

About the Artist

Rich Schwartz is a Chancellor's Professor of Mathematics at Brown University. These games were originally part of his extensive collection of Java applets illustrating deep concepts in geometry and dynamic systems.
