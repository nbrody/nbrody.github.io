Silver's Turn
Click a piece to select it.

How to Play

Move or rotate your pieces to redirect your laser and hit the opponent's Pharaoh.

Each turn, choose one action: move a piece to an adjacent square, swap with a Scarab, or rotate a piece 90°. The laser fires automatically.

Pieces

♛
Pharaoh — Lose if hit by laser
◈
Sphinx — Fires laser. Rotate only
△
Pyramid — Mirror deflects 90°
⬡
Scarab — Dual mirrors. Immune. Swaps
⛊
Anubis — Front immune, sides/back not
