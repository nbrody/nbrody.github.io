# UC Santa Barbara — Sources Consulted

URLs used when researching UCSB for `ucsbCampus.js` and `santaBarbara.js`.
Retrieved 2026-04-21.

## Primary

- [UCSB campus — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_California,_Santa_Barbara_campus)
  — Campus divided into four parts (Main/East, Storke, West, North);
  lagoon in southernmost section; UCen "perched on a hill above the
  lagoon"; cliffs directly above the Pacific.
- [Storke Tower — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Storke_Tower)
  — GPS `34°24′45″N 119°50′54″W` (= 34.4125, -119.8483); height 175 ft /
  53 m; 61-bell carillon specs; "center of the UCSB campus overlooking
  Storke Plaza."
- [University Center — Clio](https://theclio.com/entry/117924)
  — UCen is "situated with a view over the lagoon and towards the ocean,
  south of, and between the Music Building and the Arts Building." The
  3-story building was "placed alongside the campus lagoon."

## Topography & geology

- [Tectonic geomorphology of marine terraces: Santa Barbara fold belt (U. Cincinnati PDF)](https://webcentral.uc.edu/eprof/media/attachment/eprofmediafile_2435.pdf)
  — Academic paper on the marine-terrace sequence that UCSB sits on.
- [UCSB Beach Cliff Rockfalls — Sylvester / UCSB Geology](https://sylvester.faculty.geol.ucsb.edu/UCSB_Beaches/cliff%20erosion3.html)
  — Quantitative cliff-height and rockfall data.
- [UCSB Shoreline Adaptation Project — Office of Budget & Planning](https://bap.ucsb.edu/capital-and-physical-planning/sea-level-rise-adaptation-strategy/shoreline-adaptation-project)
  — Sea-level rise planning and cliff-retreat projections.
- ['Everything's Getting Pinched': UCSB and Isla Vista Grapple with Constricting Coastline — Santa Barbara Independent](https://www.independent.com/2026/04/17/everythings-getting-pinched-ucsb-and-isla-vista-grapple-with-constricting-coastline/)
  — Context on ongoing shoreline erosion.
