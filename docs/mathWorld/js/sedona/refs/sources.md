# Sedona Sources

Research date: 2026-04-29

- USGS Water Data / Water Quality Portal, Oak Creek at Red Rock Crossing near Sedona, AZ, site USGS-09504440. Used for Red Rock Crossing coordinates and 3,950 ft site elevation.
- TopoQuest / USGS GNIS, Cathedral Rock, AZ. Used for Cathedral Rock summit coordinates and 4,921 ft elevation.
- Peakbagger, Cathedral Rock, Arizona. Cross-check for summit elevation, prominence, and coordinates.
- LatLong.info, Sedona, Arizona. Used for city center coordinates and general elevation.
- FAA airport data / Sedona Airport references. Used for Airport Mesa/Sedona Airport elevation and coordinates.
- Maplogs elevation pages for Airport Mesa and Chapel of the Holy Cross. Used as secondary elevation checks.
- USGS US Topo 7.5-minute map for Sedona, AZ, 2018. Used as the general topographic provenance for local contours, hydrography, and named features.
- Arizona Geological Survey / Devil's Kitchen Sinkhole references and GeoHack. Used for Soldier Pass sinkhole coordinates and the approximately 4,510 ft rim elevation.
- Coconino National Forest, Long Canyon Trail No. 122. Used for Long Canyon Trailhead GPS coordinates, trail setting, and 4,558 ft trailhead elevation.
- Hiking references for Birthing Cave via Long Canyon Trail. Used for the side-trail description, approximate route length, and short steep final climb.
