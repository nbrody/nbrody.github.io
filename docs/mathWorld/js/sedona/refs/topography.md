# Sedona Topography Notes

Research date: 2026-04-29

## Anchors

- Sedona city center: 34.86974, -111.76099, elevation about 1,323 m / 4,341 ft.
- Oak Creek at Red Rock Crossing, USGS site 09504440: 34.8244629, -111.8062666, elevation 3,950 ft / 1,204 m.
- Cathedral Rock summit: 34.82002, -111.79321, elevation about 4,921-4,967 ft / 1,500-1,514 m.
- Sedona Airport / Airport Mesa: about 34.85, -111.79, elevation about 4,830 ft / 1,472 m.
- Chapel of the Holy Cross: about 34.83199, -111.76676, elevation about 4,439 ft / 1,353 m.
- Devil's Kitchen Sinkhole: about 34.8864, -111.7825, rim elevation about 4,510 ft / 1,370 m. It sits on Soldier Pass Trail.
- Long Canyon Trailhead / Birthing Cave access: 34°54'24.0"N, 111°49'27.5"W, trailhead elevation about 4,515-4,558 ft. Birthing Cave is reached by following Long Canyon Trail and turning onto a short left-side social trail with a final steep climb.

## Shape Used In Code

- The explorable level is centered on Oak Creek at Red Rock Crossing, because it gives a recognizable view of Cathedral Rock and a strong terrain contrast.
- Oak Creek is modeled as a shallow north-south meander on the west side of the playable scene.
- Cathedral Rock is compressed into a cluster of red sandstone spires east/southeast of the creek. The real summit is roughly 1.2 km east and 0.5 km south of the creek site; the game compresses this into a walkable foreground.
- Airport Mesa is represented in the regional terrain as a raised tabletop southwest of Sedona.
- The regional terrain uses dry washes, red sandstone shelves, and pinyon-juniper vegetation bands rather than a coastal/ocean model.
- Devil's Kitchen and Birthing Cave are both north/northwest of the original Red Rock Crossing anchor in the real world, too far apart for literal 1:1 walking scale. The level compresses them into the northern half of the tile so Sedona starts at Devil's Kitchen and offers a walkable Birthing Cave branch while keeping Oak Creek and Cathedral Rock reachable.
