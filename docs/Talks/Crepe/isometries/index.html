Isometries

Isom(ℝ)

Isometries of the line are translations and reflections.

Isom(ℝ²)

Translations, rotations, reflections, and glide reflections.

SO(3)

Rotations of the sphere, specified by axis and angle.

Isom(ℍ²)

Hyperbolic translations, rotations, and parabolic isometries in the Poincaré disk.
