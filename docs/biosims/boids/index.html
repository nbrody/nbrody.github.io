Boids

Each agent steers using three local rules — separation, alignment, and cohesion — scanning only its nearby neighbours. No leader, no global plan; flocks just emerge. Drag on the canvas to scatter the flock.

Controls

About

Boids (Reynolds, 1987) demonstrate that complex collective motion can arise from simple, local interactions. Drag your cursor to scare the flock; release to let them re-form.
