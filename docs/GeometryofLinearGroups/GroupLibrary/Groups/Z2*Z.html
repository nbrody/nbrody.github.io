A representation of \(\mathbb{Z}^2 * \mathbb{Z}\)

Consider the matrix \(X=\begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & -1 & -1 \end{pmatrix}\) in \(\mathsf{SL}_3(\mathbb{Z})\). We also have \(Y=X^2-2I=\begin{pmatrix} -1 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix}\in \mathsf{SL}_3(\mathbb{Z})\)
