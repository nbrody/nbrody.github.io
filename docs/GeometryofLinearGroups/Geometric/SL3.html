How To Read The Picture

  • Colored vertices are lattice classes, with color determined by type modulo 3.
  • The faint chamber mesh is generated from lines and planes in L / pL around orbit vertices.
  • The bright path tracks the reduced word currently applied to the basepoint.
  • Click a vertex to inspect its canonical upper-triangular representative.

Model

A vertex in the building of PGL_3(Q_p) is a homothety class of rank-3 lattices in Q_p^3. This viewer keeps a canonical upper-triangular representative for each lattice class and uses your matrices to act on the base lattice.

Neighborhoods

Around a lattice L, adjacent vertices come from nontrivial proper subspaces of L / pL. For rank 3 those are lines and planes, and chambers come from flags line ⊂ plane.

Animation

Generator buttons append or cancel letters in a reduced word. The highlighted tracker shows the corresponding orbit path for the basepoint. Sequence animation accepts tokens such as g1 and g2^-1.
