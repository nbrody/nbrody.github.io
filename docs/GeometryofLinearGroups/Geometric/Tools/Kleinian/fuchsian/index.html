How it Works

This tool visualizes the Dirichlet domain in the Poincaré disk model of hyperbolic 2-space.

  1. Enter a set of 3D vectors [x, y, t] above.
  2. Each vector v must be spacelike (i.e., x²+y² > t²).
  3. You may also enter lines like (a, b, c, d). These are interpreted as 2×2 real matrices \begin{pmatrix}a & b \\ c & d\end{pmatrix} in PSL(2,ℝ); we map them to SO(2,1) and compute their geodesics automatically.
  4. The final coordinate t must be nonpositive (<= 0).

The point (0,0,1) in Minkowski space R^{2,1} corresponds to the center of the disk.
