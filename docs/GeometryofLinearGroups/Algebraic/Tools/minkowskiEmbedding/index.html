Minkowski Embedding of \(\mathbb{Z}[\frac{1}{3}]\)

This visualization shows how to separate points in \(\mathbb{Z}[\frac{1}{3}]\) in a product of local fields. Initially, we see \(\mathbb{Z}[\frac{1}{3}]\) as a dense subring of \(\mathbb{R}\). The animation spreads points according to their 3-adic valuation, mapping \(x\) from \((x, 0)\) to \((x, 3^{v_3(x)})\).
