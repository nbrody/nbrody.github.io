Cellular Automaton

Conway's Game of Life

A zero-player game on an infinite grid. Each cell lives, dies, or is born based on how many of its eight neighbours are alive. Click or drag on the grid to paint cells; drop in a preset pattern and watch it evolve.

Controls

Choosing a pattern clears the board and stamps it in the centre.

Generation 0
Alive 0
Rules

A live cell with 2 or 3 live neighbours survives; any other neighbourhood kills it. A dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbours becomes alive. That's it — yet it's Turing complete.
