About the method

Stam's stable fluids splits each step into four pieces: add forces, diffuse via implicit Gauss–Seidel, advect by tracing each cell back along the velocity field (semi-Lagrangian), then project the result onto the divergence-free subspace by solving a Poisson equation for pressure. The same solver is reused to advect a passive dye field for visualization. Vorticity confinement adds a small curl-amplifying force to compensate for numerical diffusion of small eddies.
